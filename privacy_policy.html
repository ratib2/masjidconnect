Privacy Policy

App name: MasjidConnect
Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MasjidConnect ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

MasjidConnect collects the following data:

  • Precise location (GPS): We access your device's GPS location to find mosques and Islamic centers near you.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Location

Your GPS location is used exclusively to:

  • Show mosques and Islamic centers near your current position.
  • Calculate distances between you and nearby mosques.
  • Display prayer times relevant to your location.

Your location is processed on-device and is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

3. Location Permission

The app requests location permission only when you actively use features that require it. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time through your device settings. If you deny location access, you can still use the app by entering your location manually.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. No analytics services, advertising networks, or external APIs receive your location data.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store your location on our servers, there is no personal data to retain or delete.

6. Children's Privacy

MasjidConnect is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
